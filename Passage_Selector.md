Passage Selector
A passage/dialogue is a good candidate if 2 or more apply:
1.	Expectation Inversion – what should matter doesn’t
2.	Status Mismatch – characters misjudge who has power
3.	Tone Collision – emotional tones don’t line up
4.	Anthropomorphic Projection – animals/objects act socially
5.	Audience Knows More – dramatic or ironic advantage
6.	Over-Seriousness – trivial thing treated as critical (or vice versa)
7.	Compressed Worldview – a character’s entire outlook is revealed in seconds
